Neymar Jr is a Brazilian wonder boy who plays football. He is a winger and mostly plays on the left. His full name is Neymar da Silva Santos Junior. He is one of the best players in the world. He is an all-round player full of dribbling, play-making, and goal-scoring skills.

He was born to his mother Nadine Santos and a former Brazilian football player Neymar Santos Sr. He is now a businessman and entrepreneur. Neymar also has a sister named Rafaella Santos. The two share a very close bond.

Neymar Jr. has a son named David Lucca da Silva. Neymar became a father at a very young age.
